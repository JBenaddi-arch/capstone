What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. It aims to secure sensitive information from hackers.

Common Cyber Threats

Cybercriminals use various methods to attack systems. The most common threats include malware (harmful software like viruses) and phishing, which uses fake emails to steal private data.

Image2 text

How to Stay Safe Online

Protecting yourself is simple. You should always use strong, unique passwords for your accounts, enable two-factor authentication (2FA), and never click on suspicious links.
